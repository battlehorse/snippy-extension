SnipBin is a repository of snippets collected and shared by web users.

Whenever you browse the web, you always stumble upon bits of information that you would like to keep around for future reference. Maybe you are collecting shopping ideas, or references for your research work, or blog citations about your most recent achievements.

Whatever your objective is, SnipBin is the best way to collect snippets from webpages, save them together and publish them on the web for your friends to see!

SnipBin is tightly integrated with Chrome, a new web browser from Google. You need to have Chrome installed in order to collect and upload snippets, but you can use any other browser to navigate this website and see what other people have collected and shared.

How it works?

First, you install the Snippy Chrome Extension. Extensions are small components that augment the functionalities of the Chrome browser.

Once installed, you use the extension to grab snippets and parts of web pages. You can collect as many snippets as you want, organize and delete them. If you want you can keep them on your machine only, or you can upload them to SnipBin, where you can keep them for future reference, or share them with the world!

Known limitations

Snippy doesn't capture videos, applets and other rich content.

Snippy tries its best to capture all the contents you have choosen, but it is still unable to capture content provided by embedded plugins, such as flash animations, videos, applets and some other kinds of rich content.

Snippy is not enabled for some websites.

For security reasons, Snippy is not enabled for secured webpages (pages that are served with the https protocol), and a few other minor cases (local content, non-http URIs). It won't probably work, for instance, on your bank account internet portal.

Snippy grabbed some content, but it's no longer there and/or is not available to others I've shared it with.

Snippy copies the contents of the page you're looking at as they are. In particular, it hotlinks all the external resources, such as images. Contents that live in protected areas available only to you (such as your personal mail or your Facebook profile) may not be available to other people (or even to you when you log out of such websites) when you grab them with Snippy.
